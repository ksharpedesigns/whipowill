Whip-O-Will Campsites offers lakeside camping and a world of wilderness on 100 acres of New York’s beautiful and historic Northern Catskill Mountains. Whether your idea of camping is pulling into a full-hookup site, or sleeping under the stars in our lakeside tent area, Whip-O-Will Campsites has what you’re looking for. Fish in our 7-acre lake (no permit required) or relax by the pool.
